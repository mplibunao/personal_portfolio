From Video Games to Gamified To-do lists

Earlier this year (around January which incidentally was a few weeks before I started Free Code Camp), I found this gamified to-do app called Habitica. You get a character, with quests, skills and items. Basically all the elements in a typical rpg game and progress or regress through the game by doing your dailies, habits and to-do's. In an instant, I was hooked! With some aid from my strong imagination, I was able to become more productive while still satisfying my gamer needs. Then I discovered this pomodoro timer called ForestApp which lets you plant trees and gives you gold for every Pomodoro session you do. Anyway I'll let you check it out if your interested but if you're a gamer like me, you should try out gamification because it might just change your life :)
